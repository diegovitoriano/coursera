Friends

Rachel

Rachel's personality evolves somewhat over the series. Early on, she is portrayed as a spoiled girl. She can often be self-centered, but is often sweet and tries to be a good friend. Later on in the series she becomes much less self-absorbed.

Monica

While she is kind, caring and friendly, Monica is perhaps most notorious for her obsessive cleanliness. She is also known to talk abnormally loudly and for her bossiness and unnatural organizational skills.

Phoebe

Phoebe is a sweet-natured but odd guitar player. Having experienced nothing like a normal, complete childhood, Phoebe is often very dim-witted, sometimes smart and is in some regards still a child at heart.
